Goncharova configurations
Quadruples of residues mod p (p=4k+1). Each of the 11 graph types sounds as a four-voice chord (the four residues); every pair whose difference is a quadratic residue adds a faint "glue" voice — so the lattice of agreeing differences is audible, and each type sounds distinct. In memory of Lydia Goncharova.
—
Chord order follows the QR-rhythm of the trigger position: at a residue position chords climb low→high; at a non-residue they fall high→low. ⇅ and the global reverse flip it.
